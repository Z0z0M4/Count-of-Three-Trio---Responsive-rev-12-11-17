Frequently Asked Questions:

What kind of events do you play?

So far, we have played for wedding ceremonies, cocktail hours, private parties, birthday parties, graduation functions, and even hospital wards! We are always looking for new performing opportunities, so please feel free to contact us about playing at your event!

What materials do you require at events?

Three armless chairs and adequate lighting. We’ll bring the rest.

Do you perform outdoors?

Yes! However, during inclement weather conditions, we do ask that an awning/tent (or any other similar covering) be provided, so we can protect our instruments.

What are the most common wedding pieces that you perform?

See our repertoire list for our most commonly performed wedding pieces (marked with an asterisk*).

Can you play modern pieces or "popular music"?

Yes! We always try our best to cater to the musical interests of our clients.

Can you arrange a particular piece or song I like?

Yes (for an additional fee). If you can provide sheet music and/or a recording of the requested piece/song, that would be very helpful as well!

How far in advance do I need to make a request for a musical arrangement?

For the highest quality, requesting the piece 6-8 weeks before the event (or earlier) would be desired. For any completed arrangement, we require at least 2 weeks notice before the event.

Do musical arrangements cost extra?

Yes. The cost of an arrangement depends on the length of the piece and the complexity of the arranging.

Are you open to performing with other musicians?

Absolutely! We have performed with other musicians in the past (from alto saxophone to electric guitar), and have had a blast doing so! Whether you know of a friend/relative, or would like us to bring in our own colleagues (for an additional fee), we are definitely willing to perform with anyone (and any instrument) to make your event memorable!

How long can you play?

We can play as long as desired, with 5-10 minute breaks within each hour.

How far in advance should we book?

2 months before the event (or earlier) is recommended. However, when it comes to booking last-minute events, we are certainly not opposed to the idea – contact us to find out if we’re available!

What do you wear?

We are capable of matching our attire to suit your particular event (dress shirts, dress shoes, suit jackets, dresses, tuxedos, gowns, ties, bowties, vests, fedoras, a veritable plethora of colors, etc.). If you have a particular preference, please let us know.

How much do you charge?

Our rates vary, depending upon numerous factors (length/type of performance, musical arrangements, travel expenses, extra musicians, etc.). We will discuss particular fees with you during your initial consultation.
